E-community (LMS) - 2023

E-community (e-com) is a website for the benefit of both students and faculty staff members. It provides a set of comprehensive features to support students to register for courses, chat with Professors and TAs, notifications for every important announcement, and viewing their grades and exam halls, get to know the schedule and more. Professors and teaching assistants can view the number of students registered in every course, statistics for students, fill-in attendance sheets among other features.

Technologies

Angular, Bootstrap, Laravel, MySQL
